Wheel Alignment & Balancing

Ensure a smooth, safe ride with proper alignment.

Schedule this service

Overview

Misaligned wheels can cause uneven tire wear, poor handling, and reduced fuel economy. Our precision alignment and balancing services correct your vehicle's suspension angles and balance your tires for a smooth, vibration-free ride.

What's included

  • Extends tire life significantly
  • Improves steering and stability
  • Saves on fuel costs
  • Reduces vibration and noise

How it works

1

Inspection

We check your current alignment and balance.

2

Adjustment

We adjust camber, caster, and toe to manufacturer specs.

3

Test Drive

We verify the alignment and ensure a smooth ride.

Common questions

How do I know if I need an alignment?

Signs include uneven tire wear, pulling to one side, or a crooked steering wheel.

How long does alignment take?

Typically 30-60 minutes depending on the vehicle.
