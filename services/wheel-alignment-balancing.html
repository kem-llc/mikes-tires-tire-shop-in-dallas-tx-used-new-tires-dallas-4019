Wheel Alignment & Balancing

Precision alignment and balancing for a smoother ride.

Schedule this service

Overview

Proper wheel alignment and balancing extend tire life and improve handling. Our state-of-the-art alignment system ensures your vehicle meets manufacturer specifications. We also offer computerized wheel balancing for a vibration-free ride.

What's included

  • Extended tire life and even tread wear
  • Improved fuel efficiency
  • Smoother, safer driving experience
  • Prevents steering and suspension issues

How it works

1

Inspection

We check tire pressure, tread wear, and suspension components.

2

Alignment

Using laser alignment tools, we adjust camber, caster, and toe angles to spec.

3

Balancing

Each wheel is balanced to eliminate vibrations and ensure a smooth ride.

Common questions

How often should I get an alignment?

We recommend an alignment every 6,000 miles or when you notice uneven tire wear or pulling.

Will alignment fix a crooked steering wheel?

Yes, alignment often corrects a crooked steering wheel. We'll adjust it to center.
