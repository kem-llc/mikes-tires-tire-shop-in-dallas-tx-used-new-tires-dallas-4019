Overview

Don't let a flat tire ruin your day. Our technicians can repair most punctures and leaks quickly. We use industry-standard patch and plug methods to ensure a lasting repair.

How it works

1

Inspection

We locate the puncture and assess if it's repairable based on size and location.

2

Repair

We patch and plug the tire from the inside for a secure, long-lasting fix.

3

Common questions

Can all punctures be repaired?

Punctures in the tread area up to 1/4 inch can usually be repaired. Sidewall damage is not repairable.

How much does a tire repair cost?

We offer flat-rate tire repair starting at $20. Call us for an exact quote.
