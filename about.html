Our story

Your Dallas Tire Experts Since 1995

To provide safe, affordable tires and exceptional service to every customer who walks through our doors.

Mike's Tires was founded in 1995 with a simple mission: offer quality tires at fair prices without the high-pressure sales tactics. Over the years, we've grown from a small shop into a trusted destination for drivers across Dallas. Our commitment to honesty and hard work has earned us a loyal customer base.

Today, we continue that tradition. Every tire we sell is inspected, every repair is done right, and every customer is treated like family. We're proud to serve the Dallas community and look forward to helping you for years to come.
