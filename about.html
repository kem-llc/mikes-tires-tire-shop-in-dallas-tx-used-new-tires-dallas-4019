About Mike's Tires

To provide Dallas drivers with high-quality tires and expert service at fair prices, ensuring safety and satisfaction on every visit.

Mike's Tires was founded in 2008 with a simple mission: offer reliable tires and honest service to the Dallas community. What started as a small shop has grown into a trusted destination for drivers across the city, thanks to our commitment to quality and customer care.

Over the years, we've served thousands of customers, built lasting relationships, and earned a reputation for integrity. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest tire technology and service techniques to provide the best possible experience. We're proud to be a locally owned and operated business that puts customers first.
