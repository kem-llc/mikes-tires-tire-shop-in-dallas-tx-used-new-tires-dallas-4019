About Mike's Tires

To provide Dallas drivers with safe, affordable tires and exceptional service every time.

Mike's Tires was founded with a simple mission: offer quality tires at fair prices without the high-pressure sales tactics. What started as a small shop has grown into a trusted destination for drivers across Dallas. Our team brings decades of combined experience, and we treat every customer like a neighbor.

We believe in transparency, honesty, and hard work. From our used tire inspection process to our price match guarantee, every aspect of our business is designed to put you first. Whether you need a budget-friendly used tire or the latest Michelin, we've got you covered.
